What Is the World Reading?

Explore global reading trends through interactive visualizations. From publication history and popular genres to the languages books are written in, this site offers a snapshot of what the world has been reading.

Data sourced from a curated Goodreads dataset provided by UC San Diego

How many books has the world been reading?

This area and line chart shows how many books were published each year, based on a sample of 1,024 titles. It uses original publication year data to highlight historical trends in global book publishing. Hover over each circle to see the exact number of books published that year.

What kind of books is the world reading?

The word cloud below visualizes the most popular book genres from a dataset of 1,033 titles. Each word represents a top-placed bookshelf or genre tag, with larger text indicating greater popularity. Refresh the page to generate a new layout with the same data.

Where are the books coming from?

This map shows the languages in which 1,026 sampled books were originally published, mapped to their corresponding countries. Countries are shaded based on the number of books published in their primary language. Hover over a country to see its book count.
